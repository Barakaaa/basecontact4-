about us

ICT DEPARTMENT

Basecontact Company Limited, established in 2008 with registration number 140258 and TIN number 135-716-960, is a leading IT and ICT services provider based in Mwanza, Tanzania. The company specializes in a wide range of services, including maintenance and repair of office equipment, tender and supply of ICT accessories, website and graphic design, printing, CCTV camera installation, electrical fencing, biometric fingerprint systems, networking, intercom systems, automatic gate operators, GPS car tracking, and management systems. With a team of highly skilled professionals, each with extensive experience in various IT and ICT fields, Basecontact prides itself on delivering top-tier services tailored to meet the specific needs of its clients. Our management fosters a collaborative environment, ensuring that team members excel in their respective roles. By combining business expertise with advanced technology capabilities, we help businesses align their IT and ICT strategies with their overall vision. Our approach allows us to build scalable, high-quality business solutions using the latest cutting-edge technologies, driving success and innovation for our clients.

MINERALS DEPARTMENT

Basecontact Company Limited is a dynamic enterprise specializing in comprehensive mineral exploration and research, focusing on a wide range of valuable minerals such as Tantalum, Quartz, Sapphire, Silver, and Gold. Our commitment to innovation and precision in the mineral sector allows us to conduct thorough geological surveys, prospecting, and feasibility studies aimed at identifying high-potential mining opportunities. With a strong focus on sustainable and responsible mining practices, Basecontact actively engages with investors to create strategic partnerships that drive economic growth and development in the mineral industry. Through our cutting-edge research and global network, we provide investors with reliable data and insights, enabling informed decision-making and ensuring successful mineral ventures.
